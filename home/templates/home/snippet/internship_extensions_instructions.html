Internship Extensions Instructions

If your intern needs an internship extension:

  • Note how long of an internship extension you currently have - {{ intern_selection.get_internship_extension_amount_in_weeks }} week(s)
  • Decide how many more weeks the internship should be extended
  • In the box below, pick the extension option for the total number of weeks the internship will be delayed
  • Example: Intern Foo does not currently have an internship extension. Their mentor, Bar, wants to extend the internship for 1 week. Bar picks the option to delay the stipend payment by 1 week total.
  • Example: Intern Foo had a 2 week internship extension. Their mentor, Bar, wants to extend the internship for 1 more week. Bar picks the option to delay the stipend payment by 3 weeks total.
